# GEO and AI visibility methodology

CatalogReady separates readiness from observation.

## Readiness

The discovery audit checks whether product facts are crawlable, structured and
supported by visible evidence. Evidence areas include specifications,
limitations, shipping and returns.

## Observation

Prompt packs cover discovery, comparison, value, education, trust,
limitations, use case, evidence and transaction intent. Recorded responses are
normalized into citation domains and measured using:

- target-domain citation rate;
- product mention rate;
- answer support rate when human labels are available;
- target citation share of voice;
- top cited competitor domains;
- providers represented in the snapshot.

Use repeated runs and immutable timestamps. Model outputs vary, so a single
response must not be presented as a stable market fact.
